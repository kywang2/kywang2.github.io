Biography

My name is Kyle Wang. I was born and raised in Columbus, Ohio. I went to Ohio State University and graduated in 2016 with a degree in accounting. After graduating, I worked at the 5th largest public accounting firm in the country, RSM US as an Auditor where I was responsible for ensuring the accuracy of financial statements for clients in the financial as well as manufacturing and distribution sector. I worked at RSM US for 2 years before transitioning to Cardinal Health as an Advisor in Accounting where I was responsible for performing the inventory accounting of over 50 pharmacies and hospitals around the country. After a year at Cardinal Health, I outgrew the monotony of accounting and left to pursue a dream of becoming a professional software developer and be able to lean more on my creativity and critical thinking and work more with logic and mathematics and it's been an amazing journey ever since.
